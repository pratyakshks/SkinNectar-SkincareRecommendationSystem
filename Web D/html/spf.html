Why
Use SPF?

SPF, or Sun Protection Factor, shields skin from harmful UV radiation, preventing sunburn, premature aging, and reducing the risk of skin cancer. It blocks both UVA and UVB rays, safeguarding against sun damage, including wrinkles, fine lines, and dark spots. Daily application of SPF is crucial, even on cloudy days, maintaining skin health and preserving its youthful appearance for the long term.
