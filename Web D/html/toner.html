Why
Use Toners?

Toner plays a vital role in skincare routines, balancing pH levels, and removing excess oil, dirt, and makeup residue missed by cleansers. It tightens pores, hydrates, and preps skin for better absorption of serums and moisturizers. Additionally, it can soothe and calm sensitive skin. Regular use of toner promotes clearer, smoother, and more radiant skin, making it a crucial step in skincare regimes.
